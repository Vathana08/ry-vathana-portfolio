About Me

I am a Cambodian music producer and artist blending Afrobeat, modern trap, and melodic Khmer influences. My mission is to bring Khmer music to the global stage, fusing cultural identity with modern soundscapes. I am focused on collaborations that seek commercial viability while respecting cultural roots.
